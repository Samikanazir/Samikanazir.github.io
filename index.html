About Me

Sameeka Nazir

I am a Research Scholar in Sericulture at SKUAST-Kashmir, with interests in agriculture and crop sciences, including silkworm biology, mulberry cultivation, and sustainable farming practices. Currently, my research focuses on nematology, involving field experiments, laboratory work, and data-based analysis of crops. I aim to apply modern research methods and analytical tools to strengthen agricultural and sericulture research.
